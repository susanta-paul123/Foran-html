Our Product Range Is Delivered Around the World From Our

Our raw ingredients are carefully selected to formulate a unique feed for high-performance horses. Natural high-grade wholegrain carbohydrates and proteins are at the centre of each formulation. Carbohydrates are a major source of energy, often referred to as “fast-release” or “slow-release” energy sources.

Oats, barley, maize and wheat are natural sources of protein and—when supplemented with soya—provide a range of amino acids targeted for performance and rapid recovery. Protein has various roles in the body, with requirements pertaining to both age and workload.

Pioneering the Best Industry-Standard Testing Methods and Prevention of NOPS in Partnership With Key Authorities

Our highly experienced agronomists work hand-in-hand with local and international grain producers and suppliers, ensuring that all raw materials meet strict specifications for optimal performance. We source our raw materials through contracts with only the most trusted global ingredient suppliers. Approximately 40% to 50% of our products are grown by our local growers within the region. South-East Ireland has some of the most fertile lands, with Connolly’s RED MILLS being very fortunate to have some of the best grain producers in the world providing us with premium-quality oats, barley and wheat.

We import our soya bean from America, sunflower from France, lucerne from Holland, and beet pulp from Germany, to name a few. Our contracted suppliers are aware that these materials are destined for our renowned horse feed. Only the highest standard is accepted, from processing and shipping to storage and the handling at ports, with our logistics partners being our first set of eyes. Additionally, an important role is taken on by ensuring that all raw materials meet the required standards before entering our mill.
